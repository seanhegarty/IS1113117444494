What are Cloud Services?

A cloud service is any service made available to users on demand via the Internet from a cloud computing provider's servers as opposed to being provided from a company's servers. Cloud services are designed to provide easy access to applications, resources and services, and are fully managed by a cloud services provider.

What are the advantages of Cloud Services?

Employees can work from anywhere once there is an internet connection.

Reduces business costs as Cloud computing often reduces annual operating costs for firms.

Provides enhanced security for the firm as Cloud computing prevents data loss from scenarios such as fire, theft or disaster.

Cloud computing is considered environmentally friendly as the use of paper for documentation/storage are no longer needed.
